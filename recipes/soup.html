Cabbage, Carrot and Potato Soup

Description

An easy-to-make and delicious soup with some easy to find ingredients

This dish can be best enjoyed in days of winter

Ingredients

  • 4 large carrots
  • 3 potatoes
  • 1 onion

Steps to make

Step 1

In a large stockpot, heat olive oil over medium heat.

Stir in onion and garlic; cook until onion is transparent, about 5 minutes.

Step 2

Stir in water, bouillon, salt, and pepper.

Bring to a boil, then stir in cabbage.

Simmer until cabbage wilts, about 10 minutes.

Step 3

Stir in tomatoes. Return to a boil,

then simmer 15 to 30 minutes, stirring often.
